SPACE EXPLORATION
HOW EXPLORATION HAS EVOLVED TO GO OUTSIDE OUR EARTH.

DESCRIPTION


Space Exploration is the use of astronomy and space technology to explore the Outer Space. A space outside our planet Earth.





IS IT SUSTAINABLE?


Currently, with the rockets emitting so much fuel and massive amounts of CO2 gasses, it's not as sustainable for the environment. Once rocket scientists think of different ways to enhance the rocket to make it environmentally sustainable, then yes, it is going to be sustainable. For now though, no.

History


Space Exploration has been with us for a long, long time. The first ever recorded Space Exploration related event was in October 4, 1957. Which was the Sputnik, which also started the space race. Without the space race, there wouldn't ever be a moon landing.
